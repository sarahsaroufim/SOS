Emergency Guides

    BLEEDING

      Instructions

    1. 1. Immediately apply direct pressure
    2. 2. Immobilize the injured body part
    3. 3. Raise injury above heart
    4. 4. Bandage pad firmly in place without releasing pressure
    5. 5. Do not remove or cut an embedded foreign body
    6. 6. Apply additional pad if leaking
    7. 7. Reassure the patient
    8. 8. If excessive bleeding persists, apply a tourniquet
    9. 9. Call Emergency Services

    BLEEDING

    Immediately apply direct pressure

    Use a sterile pad, a folded cloth or your hand if necessary. Apply it very firmly over the site.

    If possible, wash your hands and use latex gloves to avoid infection.

    Slide to call Emergency Services
